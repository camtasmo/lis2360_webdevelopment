What's so important about frogs? They're much more vital than you think!

Due to their highly permeable skin, biphastic lifecycle and middle-position in the food chain, frogs are a great indicator of overall ecosystem health and wellness.

Frogs are going bye-bye. Uh-oh!

Due to how reliant they are on their environment, many frog species are becoming threatened or even endangered due to climate change and deforestation. These factors are having a devastating impact on frog populations around the world, especially in tropical environments.

How can we save the little guys? By working together!

Being more aware of how we treat our environment is a great start! If we all start practicing eco-friendly ways of thinking, we may be able to save our planet along with all the frogs on it, plus many other animal species!
